About me:

Since Jan.2016: I am a Associate Research Professor at School of Computer Science ofn Software Engineering in the East China Normal University (Shanghai, China).

Aug.2013-Dec.2015: I was a Lecturer at Software Engineering Institute in the East China Normal University (Shanghai, China).

Jul.2014-Sep.2014: I was a visiting researcher at Computer Security Lab of Nanyang Technological University.

Dec.2009-Apr.2013: I was a doctoral student in LIAFA ( University Paris Diderot- Paris 7), under the supervision of Tayssir Touili

Mar.2007-Jun.2009: I was a graduate student on Software Engineering at East China Normal University.

Sep.2002-Jun.2006: I was a undergraduate student on Electronic Information Science and Technology at Ningbo University (Ningbo China).

Research Interests:
